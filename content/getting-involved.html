Getting Involved

If you are an experienced user who can help others, or if you need help with a thorny issue, join in on the support mailing list (which also has a searchable archive).

Help on the project will be welcomed in any area - development, testing, infrastructure, documentation, web design or anywhere else you have the time and inclination to offer it.

Please consider introducing yourself on the development mailing list (which has a searchable archive). Alternatively drop Costa or Phil a private mail.

Or just fork the repository and get coding. See this FAQ about building from the repository.
